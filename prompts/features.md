Here is a comprehensive list of all features in MacBoary:

**Core Functionality**

- **Clipboard History**: Automatically saves a history of everything you copy.
- **Text Support**: Captures and stores plain text snippets.
- **Image Support**: Captures and stores copied images.
- **File Support**: Captures and stores copied files, allowing you to search them by filename.
- **Background Monitoring**: Runs silently in the background to capture clipboard changes immediately.
- **Automatic Cleanup**: Automatically removes old items based on your retention settings.
- **Persistent Storage**: History is saved to disk and survives app restarts.
- **Search**: Real-time search bar to filter your history and find items quickly.

**Productivity Tools**

- **Global Hotkey**: Customizable system-wide keyboard shortcut (default: Cmd+Shift+V) to open the history window.
- **Direct Paste**: Double-click an item to paste it directly into the active application.
- **Copy to Clipboard**: Single-click an item to copy it back to your main clipboard.
- **Pinning**: Pin important items so they are never auto-deleted.
- **Clear History**: Button to wipe your entire history.
- **Smart Clear**: Hold "Option" while clicking clear to keep your pinned items while deleting everything else.
- **Permission Monitoring**: Visually warns you if the app lacks necessary accessibility permissions.
- **App Blacklist**: Disable global hotkeys in specific apps (e.g., Royal TSX) to prevent conflicts, such as when mapping Cmd to Windows key in RDP sessions.

**Customization & Appearance**

- **Window Positioning**: Choose where the panel appears:
  - Center of Screen
  - At Mouse Cursor position
- **Themes**:
  - Follow System Appearance
  - Force Light Mode
  - Force Dark Mode
- **Custom Colors**: Fully customizable color scheme:
  - Custom Accent Color
  - Custom Background Color
  - Custom Secondary Color
- **Native UI**: Built with SwiftUI for a modern, native macOS look and feel.
- **Visual Effects**: Uses standard macOS translucency (Visual Effect View) when custom colors are disabled.
- **Dock Icon**: Option to show or hide the app icon in the Dock.

**Storage & Security**

- **Local Only**: 100% offline. No data is ever sent to the cloud.
- **Encryption**: Optional AES-256 encryption using localized keys securely stored in the macOS Keychain.
- **Configurable Retention**:
  - Item Limit: Set max items to keep (50, 100, 200, 500, or 1000).
  - Time Limit: Set how many days to keep text items.
- **Storage Location**: Data is stored in the standard Application Support directory.

**System Integration**

- **Launch at Login**: Configuration option to automatically start MacBoary when you log in.
- **Menu Bar App**: Lives in your menu bar for quick access without cluttering your dock.
- **Localization**: Built-in language selection support.
  - English
  - German
  - Spanish
  - French
  - Chinese
  - Japanese
  - Korean
  - Russian
  - Portuguese
  - Italian
